    GATEWAY -->|"/api/products/**"| ECS

    %% Eureka Discovery
    EUREKA -.->|"Registry"| GATEWAY
    EUREKA -.->|"Register"| TS & HS & GS & VS
    EUREKA -.->|"Register"| BS & IS & RS
    EUREKA -.->|"Register"| TPS & ES & ECS

    %% Feign (Synchronous)
    BS ===>|"Feign: Check & Confirm"| HS
    BS ===>|"Feign: Check & Confirm"| GS
    BS ===>|"Feign: Check & Confirm"| VS
    AIS ==>|"Feign: Search"| HS & GS & VS & RS & TPS

    %% Kafka Event Flows
    BS -->|"booking-events"| KAFKA
    BS -->|"booking-notifications"| KAFKA
    KAFKA -->|"booking-events"| IS
    IS -->|"trip-completion-events"| KAFKA
    KAFKA -->|"trip-completion-events"| RS
    RS -->|"rating-update-events"| KAFKA
    KAFKA -->|"rating-update-events"| HS & GS

    %% External APIs
    AIS -.->|"LLM"| GEMINI
    AIS -.->|"Maps"| GMAPS

    %% Database connections
    TS & HS & GS & BS & RS --> NEON
    VS & IS & TPS & ES & ECS --> SUPA_DB

Architecture Legend

Client Layer
Next.js 16 frontend (port 3000)
Infrastructure
Gateway, Eureka, Kafka
Core Domain Services
Tourist, Hotel, Guide, Vehicle
Orchestration Services
Booking (Saga), Itinerary, Review
Secondary Services
Trip Plan, Events, E-Commerce
AI Layer
AI Agent (Gemini + LangChain4j)
External Services
Supabase Auth, Google APIs
Database Layer
Neon + Supabase PostgreSQL

Kafka Event Flows

booking-events
Booking Service -> Itinerary Service (3 partitions)
booking-notifications
Booking Service -> (Future Notification Service)
trip-completion-events
Itinerary Service -> Review Service (3 partitions)
rating-update-events
Review Service -> Hotel + Tour Guide (3 partitions)
